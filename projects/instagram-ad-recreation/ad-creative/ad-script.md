# Full Refit Instagram Ad Script

## Format
- **Type**: Instagram Reel (talking head, direct-to-camera)
- **Duration**: 60–90 seconds
- **Aspect Ratio**: 9:16 (1080 × 1920)
- **Target Cluster**: Knowledge-Trapped Scaler
- **CTA**: Free Revenue Operations Diagnostic

---

## Production Notes

### Visual Setup
- **Presenter**: Full Refit founder or team lead, dressed business-casual (not suit, not hoodie)
- **Setting**: Clean, professional workspace — not a studio, not a home office. Think: industrial-modern office, visible whiteboards, real work environment
- **Framing**: Close-up, chest-to-head, direct eye contact with camera
- **Equipment**: Lapel mic, natural/soft lighting, shallow depth of field on background
- **Aesthetic**: Confident, direct, no-BS — matches Full Refit's industrial precision brand

### Style Notes
- No text overlays on video (let the words do the work, add captions in post)
- No background music
- One continuous shot or minimal cuts
- Conversational pace — not rushed, not scripted-sounding

---

## Script

### Hook (0:00 – 0:05)
> "Your best salesperson is you. And that's the problem."

*[Beat — let it land]*

### Pain Amplification (0:05 – 0:25)
> "You built a business to $5, $10, maybe $20 million. You should be proud of that. But here's what nobody talks about —
> 
> Every deal still runs through you. Your team can't close without you in the room. You haven't taken a real vacation in years because things fall apart when you leave.
> 
> You didn't build a company. You built a job you can't quit."

### Twist / Reframe (0:25 – 0:40)
> "And the thing is — it's not your team's fault. They're good people. The problem is, everything they need to sell, to deliver, to make decisions — it's all trapped in your head.
> 
> You've never had to systematize it because you were always there. Until now, when being everywhere is killing your growth."

### Solution Introduction (0:40 – 0:55)
> "That's what we fix at Full Refit. We do two things:
> 
> First, we extract what's in your head and turn it into actual systems — sales processes, operational playbooks, the infrastructure your business is missing.
> 
> Second — and this is the part most consultants skip — we make your team capable of actually running those systems without you."

### CTA (0:55 – 1:05)
> "We built a free diagnostic that identifies which of five growth bottlenecks is costing you the most revenue right now. Takes five minutes. You get a specific action plan, not a pitch deck.
> 
> Link's in the bio. Find out what's holding you back."
