# Ad Strategy & Campaign Notes

## Campaign Architecture

### Ad → Landing Page → Diagnostic → Call → Client

```
Instagram Reel (talking head)
  → Landing Page (diagnostic pitch)
    → Diagnostic Form (lead capture)
      → Email: Personalized Report + Action Plan
        → CTA: Book Strategy Call
          → Sales Conversation
            → Full Refit Engagement ($2,500–$12,000/month)
```

### Why This Funnel Works for B2B

The original Vibe Marketer funnel is: **Ad → Landing Page → Stripe Checkout → Instant Access**. This works for a $199 impulse buy.

Full Refit's $2,500–$12,000/month engagement requires a different funnel because:
1. **Higher consideration**: Nobody buys $12K/month from an Instagram ad
2. **Qualification needed**: Not every business is a fit
3. **Relationship required**: B2B services sell on trust, not features
4. **Budget authority**: Multiple decision-makers may be involved

The diagnostic serves as the "value-first" bridge — it gives something genuinely useful (the report + action plan) while creating the context for a sales conversation.

## Content Calendar Integration

### Week 1: Pre-Launch
- 3 organic posts about the 5 bottlenecks (carousel format)
- 1 longer-form reel about "what got you here won't get you there"
- LinkedIn article: "The Outgrown Informal Problem"

### Week 2: Launch
- Run the talking-head reel ad (primary)
- Static image ad (A/B test)
- Organic reel: Behind-the-scenes of a diagnostic review

### Week 3-4: Optimization
- Retarget video viewers (25%, 50%, 75% watched) with testimonial ads
- Retarget landing page visitors with case study content
- A/B test hook variations

### Ongoing
- Weekly organic content about specific bottlenecks
- Monthly case study release
- Quarterly diagnostic update/refresh

## Platform-Specific Adaptations

### Instagram Reels (Primary)
- 60-90 second talking head
- Caption includes all key points
- Link in bio to landing page
- Use Reels-specific hashtags

### Facebook Feed
- Same video, cropped to 1:1 for feed
- Longer copy in the ad text
- Direct link CTA button

### LinkedIn
- Same script adapted for LinkedIn article format
- Professional tone, data-forward
- Direct message follow-up for commenters
- Sponsored content targeting by title/industry

### YouTube Shorts
- Same 60-second cut
- End card linking to full diagnostic video (5-10 min)
- Comment pinning with link

## Key Metrics to Track

| Metric | Target | Notes |
|--------|--------|-------|
| CPM | $15-25 | B2B Instagram typical |
| Click-through rate | 1.5-3% | Higher for talking head |
| Landing page → form start | 25-40% | Diagnostic format converts well |
| Form completion rate | 60-80% | Keep it short (4 fields) |
| Cost per lead | $25-75 | B2B diagnostic lead |
| Diagnostic → strategy call | 15-25% | Qualified leads only |
| Strategy call → client | 20-30% | High-intent from diagnostic |
| Effective CAC | $500-1,500 | Acceptable for $2,500+/month |

## Creative Testing Matrix

| Variable | Variant A | Variant B | Variant C |
|----------|-----------|-----------|-----------|
| Hook | "Your best salesperson is you" | "If your business can't survive 2 weeks without you" | "90% of $5-30M companies have the same invisible problem" |
| Presenter | Founder | Team member | Split-screen client story |
| CTA | "Free diagnostic" | "Find your bottleneck" | "5-minute assessment" |
| Thumbnail | Face close-up | Text overlay on dark bg | Before/after stats |

Test in 2-week sprints. Kill losers fast, scale winners to $200-500/day.
