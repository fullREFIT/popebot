# Instagram Ad & Landing Page Recreation for Full Refit

## Project Overview

This project analyzes a successful Instagram ad and landing page from The Vibe Marketer (@thevibe410), then recreates both assets adapted for **Full Refit** — a B2B consultancy serving "Outgrown Informal" business leaders ($5M–$30M revenue) who need sales systems, AI enablement, and team capability building.

## Executive Summary

### What We Analyzed
- **Instagram Reel**: @thevibe410 post (DU0jF0UAHjb) — a talking-head video ad promoting a $199 "Vibe Skills" pack for AI-assisted marketing
- **Landing Page**: thevibemarketer.com/get-skills-pack — dark-themed, conversion-optimized sales page with social proof wall, layered skill presentation, and one-time purchase CTA

### What We Built for Full Refit
- **Target Cluster**: Knowledge-Trapped Scaler (Cluster 2) — leaders sitting on expertise that doesn't scale because it lives in their heads, not in repeatable systems
- **Product**: "Revenue Operations Diagnostic" — a free assessment lead magnet that identifies which of the 5 growth bottlenecks is costing them the most, leading into Full Refit's $2,500–$12,000/month engagement
- **Ad Format**: Talking-head reel (60-90 seconds) with direct-response hook
- **Landing Page**: Dark, industrial-themed diagnostic page with B2B social proof, pain-point architecture, and calendar booking CTA

### Why This Approach
The original ad works because it:
1. Leads with a specific, relatable problem ("You've been using AI wrong")
2. Shows social proof from real users with real results
3. Prices accessibly with zero friction ($199, instant access)
4. Uses a builder/maker identity as qualification ("Built for builders who ship")

For Full Refit, we adapt this by:
1. Leading with a specific operational pain ("Your best salesperson is you. That's the problem.")
2. Using diagnostic framing instead of product framing (B2B buyers need to feel understood before they buy)
3. Offering a free assessment instead of a low-ticket purchase (appropriate for $2,500–$12,000/month services)
4. Using an "Outgrown Informal" identity as qualification ("Built for leaders who've outgrown doing everything themselves")
